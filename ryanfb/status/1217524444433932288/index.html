January 15th, 245 BCE—Harendotes pays eight drachmae of beer tax (ζυτηρά) as an agent of Taëmbes. "Taëmbes" is a woman's name and the payment of beer tax in her name implies she was a brewer and/or tax collector. #OnThisDay
