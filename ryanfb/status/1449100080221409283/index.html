According to Schliemann's contemporaries (1875):

1) Haug: this is Greek-in-Cyprian, θείῳ Σιγῷ, "to the divine Sigo"
2) Gomperz: this is right-to-left Greek-in-Cyprian, Ταγῷ δίῳ "to the divine [Prince]"
