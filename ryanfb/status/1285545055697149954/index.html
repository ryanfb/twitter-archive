Correction: the papyrus does not state that Aurelius Sambas was enslaved, but instead Eupraxia is described as a "paidiske" (παιδίσκη) of the estate owner, Kyrillos. The term is somewhat ambiguous in this period, but I would argue that any "unfree" status is enslavement.
