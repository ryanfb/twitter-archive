Let's take a look at this: Ἐλῶν Μύσκος

ἐλῶν - driving, setting in motion (from ἐλαύνω)
μύσκος - obscure, but Hesychius defines this as "μίασμα. κῆδος" ("pollution. suffering", cf. English "miasma")

So "Elon Musk" means "driving pollution/suffering" in Greek. https://twitter.com/Atticist/status/1039252411070771201
