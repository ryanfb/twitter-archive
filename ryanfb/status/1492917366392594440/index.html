In ancient Greek, you put (τίθημι/tithemi) things away (ἀπό/apo) into an ἀποθήκη/apotheke (storehouse).

In Latin, that's an apotheca.
In Spanish, that's a bodega.
In Old Occitan, that's a botica, which becomes a French boutique.
In English, that's an apothecary.
