δαιμόνιος
—I.voc. δαιμόνιε, δαιμονίη, mostly in the way of reproach, thou luckless wight! thou wretch! sirrah! madam!—more rarely by way of admiration, noble sir! excellent man!; also by way of pity, poor wretch!; also in an iron. sense, my good fellow! good sir!
