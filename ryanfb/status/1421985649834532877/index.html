This portion of a 3rd/4thC CE papyrus codex is one of the few surviving cookbooks in Greek from antiquity, featuring recipes for:

- fish soup
- pickled/cured meat
- lentil porridge
- sprouts w/ honey
- liver skewers
- calamus sauce
- lovage & cumin sauce
