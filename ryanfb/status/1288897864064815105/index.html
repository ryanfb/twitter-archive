The "Zenon" greeted by Lauricius is possibly a military commander (χιλίαρχος/chiliarch/tribune), who wrote a contemporary inscription in Nabataean a few miles away:

SLM ZYNWN BR QYMT KLYRK BTB L'LM
Greetings, Zenon son of Qayamat, Chiliarch, with the good forever
