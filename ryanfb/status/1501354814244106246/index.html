TIL: “mongoose”: Borrowed from Portuguese mangusto, from Marathi मुंगूस (muṅgūs), from Old Marathi 𑘦𑘳𑘽𑘐𑘳𑘭 (muṃgusa), from Telugu ముంగిస (muṅgisa). Spelling altered by folk-etymological association with “goose”. Displaced native Old English nǣderbita (“snake biter”) https://twitter.com/SarahEBond/status/1501348229316702210
