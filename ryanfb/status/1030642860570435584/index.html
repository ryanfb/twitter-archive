Did you know that Bactrian and dromedary camels can both trace their etymology to Ancient Greek?

🐫 Βακτριανός (Baktrianos), from Βακτριανή (Baktrianḗ), a place name https://pleiades.stoa.org/places/971705
🐪 δρομὰς (dromas), from δρομ- (drom-), aorist stem of τρέχω (trékhō, “to run”)
