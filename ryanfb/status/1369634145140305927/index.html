March 10th, 561 CE—#OnThisDay the pork-butcher Takous delivers 960lbs of meat to 30 bucellarii (escort troops or, literally, "biscuit-eaters"). Every bucellarius gets 30lbs each, while Comitas, the possible scribe of the receipt, gets 60lbs.
