December 30th, 166 CE—a customs receipt is written out for Sarapion #OnThisDay, stating that he's transported 5 jugs of olive oil and 4 artabai of dates on 2 cargo camels.

At the bottom of the papyrus, a clay seal depicting Apis still survives.
