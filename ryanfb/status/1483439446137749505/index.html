The Greek here is: "ὀφθῆναι γοῦν ἐν Λακεδαίμονι μόνον ἐπ᾿ ἐρημίας γελῶντα"—so "Lace-daemon" is "Λακεδαίμονι"—a.k.a. Sparta. This got split across a line and hyphenated in the print edition. Then the hyphen became part of the word in OCR.

So now Myson is in "Lace-daemon".
