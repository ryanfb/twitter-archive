The contract stipulates:

- Aurelius Serenus will make new mill parts for Apion’s bakeries, farmsteads, and oil factories
- he gets 2.5 gold solidi per part
- he gets the old worn out millstones
- 24 gold solidi penalty if either party breaks the contract
